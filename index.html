Metanoia Collection

Metanoia is a Greek word that means a deep transformation of the heart, mind, and way of life. It is more than just a change, it is a complete turning away from the old self and turning toward God. True metanoia happens when we encounter Jesus Christ. Without Him, there is no genuine renewal. Only through His grace can we experience a real change in direction, character, and purpose.
